About MagabyTeachers

MagabyTeachers is an AI-powered web application designed to support teachers, schools, and educational institutions in Africa by automating the creation of high-quality educational materials.

We understand the unique challenges African educators face—diverse languages, vast curricula, and limited time. That’s why our platform is designed to be simple, fast, customizable, and localized. With MagabyTeachers, every teacher becomes a curriculum expert.
